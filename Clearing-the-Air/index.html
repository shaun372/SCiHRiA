HOW TO PLAY

Each round presents 20 statements about tobacco, nicotine and harm reduction.

For each statement, decide:

✗ MYTH  Swipe the card LEFT, press the red button, or tap the ◄ arrow key if the statement is false.

FACT ✓  Swipe the card RIGHT, press the green button, or tap the ► arrow key if the statement is true.

Each correct answer clears one grey block from the scene, revealing the image below.

Score at least 75% to clear the sublevel and unlock the next one. Get a perfect score for a 3-star ⭐⭐⭐ mastery.

After every round a full review shows every answer with explanations and sources.

ABOUT

Clearing the Air is an educational game by SCIHRIA — Scientific Insights in Harm Reduction in Africa.

It helps players understand the difference between nicotine addiction and the harms caused by tobacco combustion — and what harm reduction means in practice.

Content is drawn from peer-reviewed sources and is subject to expert committee review before any public release.
